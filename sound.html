Instrumental, improvisational, and often string-based, my sound works are situated in the margins between tonality and noise. Experiences and ideas of environment provide consistent motivations as I explore what David Teague has called "people suiting posture to place." I'm also member of Saguache and Tilth, and I frequently collaborate with the light artist Collin Parson.

I have released solo albums on a number of independent labels, nearly all of which can be streamed in full and downloaded from my Bandcamp page. A complete discography of my solo releases is available at Discogs. Below is a sample of my work.

Additionally, I curate a private imprint called Reno Park Press.
